About Me


Hi, I'm Tyler and this is my webpage! There are many like it but this one is mine. Right now, I'm a 2nd year student attending the University of Maryland, pursuing a dual degree in Aerospace Engineering and Computer Science. In addition to my schoolwork, I work at the Space Systems Lab on campus where I write software for underwater robots. I also enjoy playing poker, coding, dancing, and watching movies... but really I love learning about everything.

I also personally believe that contributing to the sciences is one of the best achievements a human can make. It's like leaving a piece of your brain behind for the benefit of the future! But, I still have a long way to go before I can leave behind something worthwhile.

I'm not really good at talking about myself but hopefully my thoughts and actions can speak for me. Go and check out the rest of my website! More will be added to this section as soon as I find more good things about myself.
